About Us

Broward Exchange was originally formed with the struggle of the college student in mind. Relieving the stress of home clutter has never been easier - we're here to give you the peace of mind that comes with no-stress item exchange. Whether you're here to buy, sell, take, trade, or give away, our algorithms are built to ensure you get what you need and get rid of what you don't.
